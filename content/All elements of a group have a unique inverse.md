Let $G$ be a group. Have $a\in G$ be an element, and suppose that it has two inverses $a^{-1}, a' \in G$. Then we have that
$$e = a a^{-1} = a a'$$
Multiplying on the left by $a^{-1}$ (or by $a'$, whichever), yields
$$a^{-1} = a'$$
