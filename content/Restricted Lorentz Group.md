up:: [[Lorentz Group]]

A [[Lorentz Transformation]] $\Lambda$ belongs to the Restricted Lorentz Group $SO^+(1,3)$ if it's in the [[Proper Lorentz Group]] and **if does not flip the direction of time** (i.e. $\Lambda_{00} \geq 0$).
