up:: [[Invariant Interval (Relativity)|Invariant Interval]]

The proper time is defined as
$$\Delta \tau = \frac{\sqrt{\Delta s^2}}{c}$$
It is, as the invariant interval $\Delta s^2$, invariant under [[Lorentz Transformation]]s.
